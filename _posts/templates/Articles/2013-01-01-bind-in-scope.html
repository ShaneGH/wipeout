Binding to the current view model

You can bind properties of a view model to itself

<wo.contentControl myValue="{ value: 'Hello wipeout!' }" myInnerValue="myValue.value"> <template> Binding to current scope: <span data-bind="text: myInnerValue"></span> </template> </wo.contentControl> <wo.view templateId="'bind-in-scope-script-1'" />

This particular example works because myValue was bound before myInnerValue. Therefore, myInnerValue could point to a property on myValue. This method is far more useful in binding to properties explicitly defined in the view model constructor.
