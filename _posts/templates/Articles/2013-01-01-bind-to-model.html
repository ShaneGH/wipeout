Binding to the current model

Unless you are building generic components, most bindnigs will point to the model. Binding to the model is as follows

<wo.contentControl model="{ value: 'Hello wipeout!' }"> <template> Binding to current scope: <span data-bind="text: model().value"></span> </template> </wo.contentControl> <wo.view templateId="'bind-to-model-script-1'" />

The model() notation comes from the fact that model is a property on the view model which is a ko.observable
