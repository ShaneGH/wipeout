Binding to global values

The binding scope also includes the window object, so you can bind to global variables

window.exampleValue = { innerValue: "Hello wipeout!" }; <wo.contentControl model="exampleValue.innerValue"> <template> Binding to global: <span data-bind="text: model"></span> </template> </wo.contentControl> <wo.view templateId="'bind-to-global-script-1'" />
