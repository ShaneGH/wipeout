Setting the template within a template

Within wipeout it is often useful to set the template within another template, rather than by id. To do this you can use the contentControl (TOTOD link) view model.

Nested content controls: <wo.content-control> <set-template> Content control 1 <div style="padding-left: 20px;"> <wo.content-control> <set-template> Content control 2 <div style="padding-left: 20px;"> <wo.content-control> <set-template> Content control 3 </set-template> </wo.content-control> </div> </set-template> </wo.content-control> </div> </set-template> </wo.content-control> Renders: <wo.view template-id="'content-control-article-1'"></wo.view>
