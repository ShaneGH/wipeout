Bind to a property on the parents view model

You may wish to bind to someting 1 level up in the view model hierarchy. You can do this using the $parent keyword.

<wo.content-control value="{ innerValue: 'Hello wipeout!' }"> <set-template> <wo.content-control value="$parent.value.innerValue"> <set-template> Binding to a property on parent's view model: <span wo-content="$this.value"></span> </set-template> </wo.content-control> </set-template> </wo.content-control> <wo.view template-id="'bind-to-parents-view-model-script-1'"></wo.view>

You can walk further up the chain of ancestors using the $parents keyword. $parents is an array, meaning $parents[0] === $parent, $parents[1] === grand parent, $parents[2] === great grand parent, etc...
