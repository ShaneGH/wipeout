Cascading models

If the model property of a view model is not explicitly set, it recieves the model of its parent.

<wo.content-control model="{ value: 'Hello wipeout!' }"> <set-template> <wo.content-control> <set-template> Binding to a property on a cascaded model: <span wo-content="$model.value"></span> </set-template> </wo.content-control> </set-template> </wo.content-control> <wo.view template-id="'cascading-models-script-1'"></wo.view>
